About Me

I’m a technical architect, educator, and systems thinker. My work focuses on building reproducible frameworks, clean design systems, and tools that empower others to create with clarity and confidence.

I value intentional naming, readable structure, and workflows that scale. My background in chemical engineering informs my approach to technical architecture: precise, structured, and deeply reproducible.

I am a software developer and a chemical engineer and a Six Sigma Master Black Belt who has spent a career (30 years) working in semiconductor manufacturing. Now, I do software development for my own pet projects and will work with you to design a custom interface for your pet projects.
